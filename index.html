Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Our standard check-in time is 2:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out is subject to availability and may incur additional charges.

Breakfast inclusion depends on the room type and package you select. Our Superior, Executive, and all Suite categories include a complimentary buffet breakfast at our main restaurant.

Yes, we provide luxury airport transfers. Our Luxury, Presidential, and Penthouse suites include complimentary transfers. For other room types, transfers can be arranged through our concierge for a nominal fee.

Cancellations made up to 48 hours before the arrival date are free of charge. Cancellations within 48 hours or no-shows will be charged for the first night's stay.

While we love animals, we currently only allow small service animals with prior notification. Please contact our front desk for more details regarding our service animal policy.
